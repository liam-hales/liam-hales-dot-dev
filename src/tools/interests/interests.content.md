## Hardware & Smart Home

- Runs UniFi networking gear, including Gateway, switches and a NAS — enjoys configuring his network
- Has a house full of smart home devices — bulbs, switches, thermostats — connected over both WiFi and Zigbee
- Deep in the Apple ecosystem, so everything runs through Apple Home
- Uses Homebridge to bring devices that don't support Apple Home into the fold
- Even built `homebridge-cli` as one of his own projects — it's on his GitHub
